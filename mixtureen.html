Catalogue

Dry mixtures for protecting surfaces

The main purpose of the mixtures is to be used as solutions for manufacturing constructional heat insulating protective structures, such as heat pipes, heat exchangers, chambers, boilers, etc, which are located in the open air or indoors.

Asbestos replacement

Fibers are the main component and used as an reinforcing element of lining mixtures. Asbestos which has unique physical properties was previously used for this purpose. Full asbestos replacement is possible only through a mixture of other heat-resistant fibers in a certain proportion.

We offer finished mixtures which are not inferior to those containing asbestos, easy to use and more environmental friendly.

Packaging and storage conditions

Standard mixture packaging - paper watertight bags of 25 and 50 kg.

Should be stored on pallets in dry rooms, covered with a plastic wrap.

The guaranteed shelf life is 6 months starting from the production date, subject to storage conditions.
