Types of Cords IZOPACK

Cords of Cross Section Braiding

Insulating cords of cross section braiding have denser structure than those cords of other types. They are produced of square, round and rectangular sections and come in sizes from 4 to 80 mm.

The cords high density allows them to be used for hot gas systems sealant and in places where density and cord size accuracy are determining factors.

Cords with Fillers

The heat-insulating cords with fillers are same in appearance as the cords of cross section braiding, but have a softer structure. They consist of a tight outer braid and a high temperature filler, which is usually based on a glass, basalt or ceramic fiber.

They are often used in places where the cross section braiding cords application require additional, more precise refinement of the mounting seat or the insulating surface.

The cords with fillers are made of round, oval, square, rectangular or trapezoidal shape of cross section and come in sizes from 12 to 150 mm.

The cords in mesh braid

The cords of this group do not contain dense braid. They consist of a soft core based on a mineral, basal or ceramic fiber, braided with a net of high temperature thread.

These cords are widely used in construction as a joint sealing with great soundproofing features and as a heat insulator of air duct systems.

The cords in a net braid are made of round cross section of diameters from 10 to 150 mm.

How to make an order

1. Select cord yourself by exploring the catalog or the parameters table.
