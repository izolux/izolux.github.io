Braided Sleeves

The IZPOLET sleeves are made from high temperature threads of diameter up to 150 mm and wall thickness from 1 to 20 mm.

The sleeves have a flexible structure, high strength and are able to withstand temperature up to +1200 C. The main purpose of the sleeves is protection of cables, hoses and high temperature sleeves from exposure to aggressive external factors.

The inner diameter and wall thickness should be specified when placing an order.

Sleeves Coated with Silicon

The IZOPLET sleeves with heat-resistant silicon coating consist of high temperature foundation and heat-resistant silicon coating.

Coating features:
- sleeves protection from external environmental effects, soaking
- resistance to molten metal splashes
- protection from punctures, cuts and abrasion
- great chemical resistance
- dielectric features

Application temperature

The silicon coating is able to perform under a constant temperature conditions up to +300 C.

The coating is able to withstand temperatures up to +1200 C under short-term temperature jump conditions, depending on the process duration. For example, sleeves with coatings performed well during a contact with molten metal splashes.

Reflective Coated Sleeves

Sleeves, covered with metal foil are able to protect against heat caused by exposure to infrared radiation. In case of temperature increase to values critical for the reflective foil, the sleeve foundation will continue to perform protective layer function.

Sleeves and Tapes

Flexible electric cable bundles and hoses can be insulated with high temperature IZOLTEX tapes, as well as with IZOPLET sleeves.

Insulation with a tape

When using tape as an insulating material, the lack of flexibility of the tape should be taken into account. The tape may often come apart when the construction is bended, which exposes the insulating surface.

Insulation with a sleeve

The IZOPLET braided sleeves have got great flexibility and elasticity. The sleeves do not disband even when strongly bended and continue protecting the insulating surface.

Rectangular Sleeves

The rectangular sleeves are used for insulating cables in places where boxes installation is envisaged, if there’s a need for an additional protection.
