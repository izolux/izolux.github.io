Type A

The packings of type A consist of strong chemical fibers. They are highly abrasion-resistant, have great chemical and thermal resistance.

Main application fields are reactive abrasive environments such as treatment plants, chemical industry, paper and cement industry, sugar production, metallurgy and mining industry.

Type C

This broadest group includes gland packing based on carbon, graphite and other high-temperature threads. They’ve got high temperature parameters, good chemical resistance, low friction and great packing properties.

Application areas: water, steam, overheated steam, aggressive chemical environments.

Type P

The packings of type P consist of strong threads based on PTFE. They have excellent resistance to aggressive chemical environments, great sealing parameters and low friction coefficient.

Application areas: aggressive chemical, nutritional and pharmaceutical environments.

The packings of type P:

IZOLOCK P-010
IZOLOCK P-100
IZOLOCK P-020
IZOLOCK P-210

Type N

The packings of this group consist of strong threads based on string natural fibers. They have good resistance to weak chemical impacts, good tribonomic parameters and sealing features.

Application areas: water supply, food and sugar industry.

pH

Chemical resistance of gland packing is one of the most important parameters of their applicability in aggressive environments.

Half of the IZOLA company’s packing assortment is of pH from 0 to 14, meaning these packings can insulate almost any environment.

Sliding Speed

Modern high-temperature fibers with low friction coefficient and high wear resistance allow to use faster types of pumping equipment.

How to make an order

1. Select packing yourself by exploring the catalog or the parameters table.

2. Let us help you in the selection of packing. Simply tell us the initial parameters (temperature, pressure and composition of the environment, type of the equipment).

Order form: packing model + cross section size (mm) + amount (kg)
For example: IZOLOCK C-010 12x12 mm, 20 kg
